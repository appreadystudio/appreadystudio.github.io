This is the live AppReady intake form for App Store rejection-risk and launch-readiness audits. It collects the details needed to review your app without a long back-and-forth.

Access and privacy: Use read-only repo access, a zip export link, TestFlight invite, screenshots, or walkthrough videos. Do not submit production secrets, API keys, private tokens, or real user passwords. Audit materials are used only for the requested review and can be deleted after delivery on request.
